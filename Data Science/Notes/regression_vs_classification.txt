Regression
-----------
    - results in a value
    - predicts a future value of something
    - depends on previous data - the more the better
    - Note:
     ------
        - logistic regression is actually a kind of classification

    ex: predicts the value of USD $ next month

Classification
--------------
    - results in a class
    - classifies something into the available classes
    - learns from previous data - the more is not always better (unbalanced data)

    ex: classifies if an someone has a specific disease or not
